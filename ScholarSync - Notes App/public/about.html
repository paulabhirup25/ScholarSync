About ScholarSync

ScholarSync is a dedicated digital library designed for Class 11 and 12 students. Our mission is to simplify access to high-quality study materials by removing the clutter, distractions, and unreliable content often found on educational platforms.

All notes are carefully curated to ensure accuracy, clarity, and alignment with the current syllabus. The core content consists of handwritten notes prepared by IIT students or developed under the academic guidance of IITians, ensuring strong conceptual depth and exam relevance.

The platform is completely free to use and respects user privacy — no sign-ups, no tracking, and no unnecessary downloads. Just focused, reliable learning resources in one place.

How to use

  • Use the search bar on the home page to find specific topics.
  • Filter by Class or Subject to narrow down the list.
  • Click "View PDF" to open the notes directly in your browser.
